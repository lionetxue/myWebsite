About Me

Hey, I'm Lin. I am an interactive designer who strives to marry the love of design and coding.

Most recently I worked as a graphic designer for Templeton Landscape Architecture & Planning. In the past, I studied bioinformatics and developed a visualization tool to analyze gene expression data.

While at Cornell, I was lucky enough to collaborate with many talented people from various backgrounds, one such collaboration led to the winning of the 5th Ed Bacon Student Design competition.

I live in Ithaca, NY with my husband and our son, a busy toddler who constantly wants to help mommy on the keyboard.

Drop me a line if you would like to work with me.
